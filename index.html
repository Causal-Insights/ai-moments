AI Moments creates personalized AI video experiences for kids and families. Parents can turn a simple photo into a short keepsake video built around magical moments children already love.

Today, our live experiences focus on personalized Santa videos, Easter Bunny videos, and Tooth Fairy videos. Each landing page on AI Moments explains what the experience looks like, how the flow works, and where to go when you are ready to create the final video.

Trust & safety

We build these experiences for real families, so the trust story has to be clear. The product is meant to feel easy, private, and age-appropriate from start to finish.

Family-friendly design

The overall tone stays warm, gentle, and focused on joyful reactions instead of shock or confusion.

No account required

Families do not need to create an account to use the live experiences on the linked creation sites.

Photo handling is limited

Uploaded photos are used to generate the experience in real time and are not stored after processing.

What kinds of personalized videos can I create right now?
