About AI Moments

We build personalized video experiences families feel good about sharing.

AI Moments is a Canadian company focused on family-friendly AI video products. We take current AI capabilities and turn them into simple experiences for Santa, Easter Bunny, Tooth Fairy, and other magical family moments.

Our approach

We care about the reaction a family has when the finished video plays, so the experience has to feel easy, warm, and trustworthy from the first click.

That means clear flows, family-friendly creative choices, and plain-language explanations that help parents understand what they are getting before they commit to a purchase.

It also means grounding the product in practical details: no account required for the live experiences, limited photo handling, and temporary retention of the finished video so families can access their order.

We want AI to feel more human in everyday family moments: useful, gentle, and a little magical.

What matters to us

The product principles behind each experience.

Family-first product design

We shape the tone, visuals, and language around what feels reassuring and delightful for families.

Privacy-minded workflows

We aim to collect as little as possible while still delivering the finished experience families expect.

Clear expectations

We use AI to create magical moments, but we explain the process in plain language so parents know what happens next.
