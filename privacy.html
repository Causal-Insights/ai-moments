Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 13, 2026

Our commitment to your privacy

ai-moments.com is operated by AI Moments Inc., a Canadian company. We are committed to being transparent about how our experiences work and how your information is handled.

Information we do not collect

When you use our experiences, you may upload a photo. We do not store, retain, or collect the photos or images you upload. They are used solely in real time to generate your video and are not saved to our servers.

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect names, email addresses, or any other personal information in order to use our experiences.

Information we do retain

The final generated video produced from your experience is retained by ai-moments.com for up to 60 days, after which it is permanently deleted. This video does not include your original uploaded photo. It is retained solely to deliver your experience and for operational purposes during that period.

Children's privacy

Our experiences are designed to be enjoyed by families. Because we do not collect personal information of any kind from users, including children, our service is designed to be safe for use by all ages. Parents and guardians are encouraged to supervise children's use of our experiences.

Cookies and tracking

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics on this site.

Third-party services

Our experiences are hosted on separate domains (santa-video.com, easterbunny-video.com, toothfairy-video.com). Each experience may have its own applicable terms. This privacy policy applies to ai-moments.com only.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at orders@ai-moments.com.

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.
